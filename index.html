About Retrograde

Retrograde are an indie soul-pop band based in Dublin, fusing elements of disco, RnB and reggae. The 6 piece band met studying music in Dublin and since have nurtured their signature sound as shown in their latest release "Goodbye Boy". They're bound to get any crowd groovin'!
